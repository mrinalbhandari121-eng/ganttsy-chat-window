Risks & Dependencies · what we'll guard against

Risks

1. Underestimating watering needs

No hose access, poor pressure, irregular watering — your garden crisps up like a forgotten pizza slice.

2. Ignoring pests and wildlife

Rabbits, deer, raccoons, voles — without deterrents your garden becomes the local wildlife buffet.

3. Poor drainage and water pooling

Waterlogged soil kills roots. A bad slope or compacted ground can sabotage everything.

Dependencies

1. Sunlight Assessment → Plant Selection

Can't pick plants until you know how much sun the area gets.

2. Soil Test → Soil Prep / Amendments

Know your soil type, pH, drainage before adding compost or fertilizer.

3. Frost Dates → Planting Schedule

Planting window depends entirely on frost risk.

4. Wildlife Assessment → Protective Measures

Fencing/deterrents before planting, not after everything's eaten.
